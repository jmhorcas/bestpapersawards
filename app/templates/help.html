Help

Add your best paper award

Go to Add paper and load the details of your best paper award by providing its DOI/URL. If the paper's information is incorrect or cannot be automatically extracted, please fix it or fill it out.

Not Verified?

You can verify a best paper award by sending the corresponding certificate or proof (formats allowed: 'pdf', 'png', 'jpg', 'jpeg') to the Admin with the paper's DOI copied into the email's body.

Errors in paper's information

First of all: Sorry, but errors always may happen... You cannot correct errors in the repository by yourself, but you can make us aware of errors in our data via email. We will try to correct errors within a few days.
